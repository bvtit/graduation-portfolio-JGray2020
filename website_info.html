Website Information

As you may be very well able to tell, I am not the greatest web designer in the world. However, this website in its entirety is made from scratch, and while it may not be very good, I prefer it to a premade website or a template, as it stands as a show of how my skills have changed over time and how I've grown. In addition, I have complete freedom over the site and can do anything I want to with it. It's still a huge work-in-progress, but I think that even now, with its lack of content and horrible programming, I'm fine with it. In all honesty, I have a slight dispassion for HTML and web design in general, but that's more a result of the way HTML works and limits things than anything with websites in general. I hope to eventually add many more interactive and cool features to the site, such as games and bots.

Planned Features

Shogi: I plan to program in Shogi, although it would be very incomplete and poorly programmed. The main objection to this plan would be the lack of a CPU player to face (as I don't have enough experience to program a bot that would be difficult enough to face,) and as such I would have to implement networking to be able to host Shogi games on either another server or the same server as the website.

Text Rearranger: A standard project I like to do with any new programming language. I would most likely use Javascript, as it would be good practice for making other games on the site.
